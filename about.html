About

Born in Istanbul on September 17, 1977, Shain is a painter resident in Brussels since 1980. Very young he is interested in all aspects of visual art and after a few years spent in an art school, he prefers to follow his way, which confirms a very self-taught approach to his art. At first, he paints simple works, minimalist collages and stands out for its versatility and these experimental techniques. His work is mainly focused on imposing constraints and developing new processes, using the mixture of textures, colors and support.
The major feature of his work is an aesthetic search for decomposition of the human body. There is also a mixture of figurative and abstraction, and as such, the artist proposes a universe of deconstruction, reconstruction where cohabit, collages, paintings and 3D. Via these three supports we obtain a dynamic and gestural work mainly made of acrylic paint. His mastery and daring in the choice of colors, reflects the sensitivity of the artist, but also and especially the modernity of his creativity.
